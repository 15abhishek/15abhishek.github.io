eCube

Implementation & Analysis of various Control Techniques to Balance a Cube





Description: A cube-shaped Reaction wheel-based 3D Inverted Pendulum. LQR (Linear Quadratic Regulator) and PID control techniques were applied in Simulation followed by implementation on the physical robot to balance the cube on its Vertex and Edges
Tech Stack: Control Systems (LQR), Embedded Systems, CAD Modelling, C++, Lua, Mathematical Modelling (GNU Octave), Robot Simulation (CopelliaSim/V-REP)
Hardware: ESP32 microcontroller, BLDC Motors, ICM-20948 IMU
Last Update: The cube is able to balance on its vertex and edges in simulation and can balance on its edges on the actual hardware.
Future Work: The current goal is to compare different control techniques including learning-based techniques to balance the cube on its vertex and to balance the cube on its vertex on the physical prototype.
