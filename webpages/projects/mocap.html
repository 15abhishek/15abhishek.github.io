MoCap

Realtime Motion Capture System




Description: A low-cost motion capture system that can track and record spatial movements of multiple objects using IR-based markers and up to 4 cameras. The output data rate is up to 40Hz at 1080p and 70Hz at 720p. Pose estimation accuracy is up to ±0.5 mm for stationary targets and ±2.5 mm for tracking dynamic objects such as quadrotors.
Tech Stack: Computer Vision, Control Systems, ROS2, Python, CAD Modelling
Hardware: High-speed Cameras, Quadcopters, IR LEDs
