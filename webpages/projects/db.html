Self-balancing Bike

Autonomous Navigation & Balancing of a 2-wheeled Bike





Description: A reaction wheel based mini-bike that can be balanced using the LQR control technique to balance the bike both at rest and in motion. The mathematical model of the bike is based on the principle of Inverted Pendulum. Additionally, path-planning and line following algorithms were implemented for autonomous navigation.
Tech Stack: Control Systems, Embedded Systems, CAD Modelling, C++, Lua Mathematical Modelling (GNU Octave), Robot Simulation (CopelliaSim/V-REP)
Hardware: AtMega 2560 microcontroller, BLDC Motors, MPU6050 IMU, MG90s Servo Motor, DC Motor
